About Me

I am a 31 year old looking to make a career change into the field of computer programming. I was born in London have lived here for most of my life. I have undertaken a number of jobs and most recently have forged a career in video editing. Although I have enjoyed this career, I am interested in building products from the ground up and feel I am ready for a new challenge. My hobbies include playing the guitar, live music, cycling and exercising, I am also interested in films, television, good food and sports. I am deeply interested in computing and technology, I have always been drawn to computers from a very young age. Unfortunately it was less of a option to study computer science when I was at school, meaning it was a less encouraged career path. I have always felt it was my passion but I did not have the means to expand upon my knowledge, founders and coders would be the ideal place for me to take the leap from a bystander to a participant in the computer technology realm.
